<p><p>The meso-micro porous carbon materials market focuses on two key types: microporous materials, which have pore sizes less than 2 nm, and mesoporous materials, characterized by pore sizes ranging from 2 to 50 nm. Microporous materials are primarily used in applications requiring high surface area and adsorption capacity, such as gas separation and storage. In contrast, mesoporous materials offer enhanced accessibility for larger molecules, making them suitable for catalytic processes, drug delivery, and sensing applications, thus driving diverse industrial demand.</p></p>
