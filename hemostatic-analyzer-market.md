<p><p>The Hemostatic Analyzer Market includes various types of tests essential for assessing blood coagulation and detecting hemostatic disorders. Routine coagulation tests encompass Prothrombin Time (PT) and its corresponding International Normalized Ratio (INR), crucial for monitoring anticoagulant therapy. Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (APTT) and Thrombin Time (TT) evaluate the intrinsic and common coagulation pathways, respectively. Additionally, tests for Fibrinogen and D-Dimer assess clot formation and breakdown. Diagnostic Hemostasis Assays, Platelet Function Testing, and Thrombophilia Testing further aid in diagnosing bleeding disorders and risk of thrombosis.</p></p>
