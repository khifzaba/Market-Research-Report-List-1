<p><p>The Post CMP Cleaner market is segmented into acidic and alkaline types, each catering to specific cleaning needs in semiconductor manufacturing. Acidic cleaners, typically composed of ammonium fluoride or sulfuric acid, effectively remove metal contaminants and residues from silicon wafers, ensuring high purity. In contrast, alkaline cleaners, often based on potassium hydroxide or sodium hydroxide, excel at dissolving organic materials and removing particles. Both types are essential in maintaining the quality and performance of semiconductor devices post-CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing).</p></p>
