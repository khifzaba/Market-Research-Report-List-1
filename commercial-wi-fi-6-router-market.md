<p><p>The commercial Wi-Fi 6 router market is primarily segmented into dual-band and tri-band types. Dual-band routers operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering a well-rounded performance for general business needs. In contrast, tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz channel, enhancing data transmission speeds and reducing interference, making them ideal for high-demand environments like offices with numerous devices. Each type caters to different networking requirements, balancing cost and performance for businesses.</p></p>
