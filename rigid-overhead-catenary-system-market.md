<p><p>The Rigid Overhead Catenary System includes several market types: Simple Catenary, Stitched Catenary, and Compound Catenary. The Simple Catenary features a single, continuous cable providing basic support. Stitched Catenary utilizes multiple, interconnected sections for enhanced flexibility and stability. Compound Catenary combines elements of both simple and stitched designs, optimizing performance for varied terrain and load conditions. These systems are essential for efficient energy transfer and reliability in electric transportation infrastructure. Each type serves specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
