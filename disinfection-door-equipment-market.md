<p><p>The Disinfection Door Equipment market includes two primary types: Automatic Disinfecting Equipment and Semi-Automatic Disinfecting Equipment. Automatic Disinfecting Equipment operates independently to sanitize individuals or items passing through, utilizing sensors for activation and typically employing UV light or chemical sprays for thorough disinfection. In contrast, Semi-Automatic Disinfecting Equipment requires some user interaction, such as pressing a button or triggering a mechanism, providing a balance between manual control and efficient disinfection. Both types enhance hygiene in various public and private spaces.</p></p>
