<p><p>The luggage market is primarily divided into two types: hard luggage bags and soft luggage bags. Hard luggage bags are constructed from durable materials like polycarbonate or aluminum, offering enhanced protection and security for fragile items. They are typically waterproof and easier to clean but can be heavier. In contrast, soft luggage bags, made from fabric like nylon or polyester, are lighter and more flexible, often providing expandable compartments. They tend to be more affordable and allow for easier storage but may offer less protection for contents.</p></p>
