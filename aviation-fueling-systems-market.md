<p><p>Aviation fueling systems are categorized into two main types: fixed installations and portable systems. Fixed installations are permanent fueling setups typically found at airports, designed for efficiency and safety in large-scale operations. They include storage tanks, pumps, and filtration systems. Portable fueling systems, on the other hand, are mobile units that can be deployed wherever needed, offering flexibility for remote locations or temporary operations. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safe and efficient refueling of aircraft.</p></p>
