<p><p>The multiplex biomarker imaging market encompasses various assay types for simultaneous analysis of multiple biomarkers in tissues. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) assays utilize antibodies to detect specific proteins, providing insights into tumor characteristics. Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) assays employ fluorescent probes to visualize genetic material, aiding in identifying chromosomal abnormalities. Tissue Microarray (TMA) assays enable high-throughput analysis of tissue samples on a single slide, facilitating comparative studies across multiple specimens. Together, these methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning.</p></p>
