<p><p>The Human Insulin Delivery System market comprises two primary types: automatic and manual delivery systems. Automatic systems, such as insulin pumps, provide continuous and precise insulin delivery, adjusting dosages based on real-time glucose levels, which enhances user convenience and glycemic control. In contrast, manual systems involve direct injection or pen delivery, requiring patients to calculate and administer doses themselves. While manual methods offer simplicity and cost advantages, automatic systems are often preferred for their ability to optimize insulin management and improve overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
