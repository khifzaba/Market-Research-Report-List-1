<p><strong>The Short Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch – Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Rayon – Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The short carbon fiber market is categorized into three main types: PAN-based, pitch-based, and rayon-based carbon fibers. PAN-based carbon fibers are derived from polyacrylonitrile and are known for their high strength and stiffness, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Pitch-based carbon fibers, produced from petroleum or coal tar pitch, offer excellent thermal stability and are often used in high-temperature applications. Rayon-based carbon fibers, made from regenerated cellulose, are less common but appreciated for their cost-effectiveness and moderate properties.</p></p>
