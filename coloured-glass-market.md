<p><p>The colored glass market includes specialized segments such as colored float glass and colored plain plate glass. Colored float glass is produced by adding metal oxides during the manufacturing process, resulting in sheets of glass with uniform color and transparency suitable for architectural applications. On the other hand, colored plain plate glass is typically thicker and used for decorative purposes, offering a range of colors and finishes. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and interior design.</p></p>
