<p><p>The AMR sensing ICs market is divided into digital output and analog output segments. Digital output AMR sensing ICs provide a binary signal, ideal for applications requiring precise on/off triggering and easier integration with digital systems. In contrast, analog output AMR sensing ICs deliver continuous voltage or current signals proportional to the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications needing finer resolution and varying measurements. Each type serves distinct application needs, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
