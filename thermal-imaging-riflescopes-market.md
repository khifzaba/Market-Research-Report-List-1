<p><p>Thermal imaging riflescopes are advanced optical devices used primarily for observation and targeting in low-light conditions. The market can be divided into two main types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging utilizes sensors that operate at ambient temperatures, making them smaller, lighter, and more cost-effective, ideal for civilian applications. Cryogenically cooled thermal imaging employs liquid nitrogen or other cooling methods to enhance image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for military and high-performance scenarios.</p></p>
