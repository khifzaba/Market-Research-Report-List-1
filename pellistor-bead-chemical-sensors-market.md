<p><p>Pellistor bead chemical sensors are categorized into three main types: electrochemical, optical, and catalytic bead (pellistor) sensors. Electrochemical sensors detect gas concentrations through chemical reactions that produce electrical currents. Optical sensors use light absorption and scattering techniques to identify gases based on their optical properties. Pellistor or catalytic bead sensors consist of a heated bead that detects flammable gases by measuring temperature changes caused by combustion reactions on its surface. Each type serves specific applications in monitoring and detecting hazardous gases.</p></p>
