<p><p>The food-grade collagen and gelatin market primarily consists of two types: collagen peptides and gelatin. Collagen peptides are soluble proteins derived from collagen, known for their ease of absorption and use in dietary supplements, functional foods, and beverages. They enhance texture, improve gut health, and support skin elasticity. Gelatin, on the other hand, is a gelling agent made from animal collagen, widely utilized in food products like desserts, gummies, and marshmallows, providing a unique texture and stability. Both are valued for their nutritional and functional benefits in food applications.</p></p>
