<p><p>The ship lights market is categorized into three main types: halogen, xenon, and LED lights. Halogen lights offer bright illumination and are cost-effective but consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. Xenon lights provide a brighter output with better energy efficiency and a longer lifespan than halogen but are more expensive. LED lights are the most energy-efficient, offering longevity and low heat emission, making them ideal for modern vessels. Each type serves different preferences and operational needs in maritime lighting.</p></p>
