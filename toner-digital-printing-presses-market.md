<p><p>The toner digital printing presses market is categorized into two main types: dry toner presses and liquid toner presses. Dry toner presses utilize powder-based toner, providing high-quality prints and fast drying times, making them ideal for short-run and variable data printing. In contrast, liquid toner presses use a finer, liquid-based toner, allowing for vibrant colors and better consistency on various media types. Both technologies cater to diverse printing needs, from commercial to industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
