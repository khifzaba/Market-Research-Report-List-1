<p><ul><li>Soft Endoscope</li><li>Rigid Endoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound endoscope market is categorized into soft and rigid types. Soft endoscopes are flexible, allowing for easier navigation through complex anatomical structures, making them suitable for delicate procedures in areas like the gastrointestinal tract. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, provide a stable platform for clear imaging and are typically used in procedures that require direct visualization, such as in orthopedic or urological applications. Both types play crucial roles in minimally invasive diagnostics and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
