<p><p>The Patient Temperature Management Devices market encompasses two primary segments: Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to maintain or elevate a patient's body temperature, crucial in surgical and recovery settings to prevent hypothermia. In contrast, Patient Cooling Systems are utilized to lower a patient's temperature, often necessary in cases of fever, heat stroke, or neuroprotection during cardiac arrest. Both systems play vital roles in optimizing patient outcomes through effective temperature regulation.</p></p>
