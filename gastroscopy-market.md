<p><p>The gastroscopy market includes three main types: electronic gastroscopes, fiberoptic gastroscopes, and capsule gastroscopes. Electronic gastroscopes utilize advanced imaging technology for enhanced visualization and diagnostics. Fiberoptic gastroscopes, known for their flexibility and maneuverability, allow physicians to examine the upper gastrointestinal tract effectively. Capsule gastroscopes, which are swallowable devices with cameras, enable non-invasive imaging and ease of use, making them ideal for patients who may be reluctant to undergo traditional procedures. Together, these technologies enhance gastrointestinal examination and diagnosis.</p></p>
