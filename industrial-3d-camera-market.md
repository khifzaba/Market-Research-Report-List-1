<p><strong>The Industrial 3D Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial 3D Camera market is primarily categorized into two main types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture entire frames at once, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed 3D imaging in static environments, such as quality inspection and object recognition. Conversely, Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, making them suitable for high-speed applications, such as conveyor systems or continuous material inspection, where objects move past the camera rapidly. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
