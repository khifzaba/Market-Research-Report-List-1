<p><p>The busbar trunking system market is divided into two main types: aluminium and copper busbar trunking systems. Aluminium busbar systems are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective, making them suitable for various applications such as commercial buildings and industrial facilities. In contrast, copper busbar systems offer higher conductivity, better thermal performance, and durability, making them ideal for high-load applications and environments with demanding power requirements. Both types enhance electrical distribution efficiency and safety in modern power systems.</p></p>
