<p><p>The endosteal implant market primarily consists of titanium and zirconium implants, each featuring unique properties. Titanium implants are widely used due to their biocompatibility, strength, and established track record in dental and orthopedic applications. They promote osseointegration, allowing them to bond well with bone. Zirconium implants, on the other hand, offer a more aesthetic solution, mimicking natural tooth color and reducing the risk of metal allergies. Both types cater to a growing demand for effective, durable dental restorations.</p></p>
