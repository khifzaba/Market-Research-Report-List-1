Dental pulp regeneration involves restoring the vital soft tissue within the tooth, crucial for maintaining its health and vitality. Tooth enamel regeneration aims to repair the protective outer layer, improving aesthetics and preventing decay. These advancements seek to enhance dental health and longevity, addressing various oral conditions through innovative regenerative techniques.</p></p>
